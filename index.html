The Problem

Spending on growth but still cannot tell what creates pipeline.

Paid is running. Content is publishing. SEO is producing traffic. Email sequences are live. But the channels are not connected to each other, attribution stops at last click, and CRM source data is incomplete for a significant portion of pipeline.

The result: budget decisions are made on last-click data that over-credits the final touch and hides the channels that created the opportunity. CAC climbs. The explanation is unclear. More budget gets added to a system that is already fragmented.

In many audits, last-click reporting misrepresents which channels are actually building pipeline. The final touchpoint gets credit. The channels that created awareness, intent, and re-engagement get none. Budget follows the wrong signal.

Fragmented system

What most B2B SaaS revenue systems look like right now

  • Paid, SEO, content, and email running independently with no shared audience signal
  • Attribution stops at last click: multi-touch channel contribution is invisible
  • Landing pages generic, not matched to ICP, segment, or buying intent
  • CRM source fields empty on a meaningful portion of pipeline and closed deals
  • CAC climbing without a clear understanding of which channels are responsible
  • No weekly review connecting spend to pipeline: optimization reactive and ad hoc

Connected system

What the same stack looks like when the system works

  • Paid, SEO, content, and email coordinated around shared ICP segments and buying signals
  • Multi-touch attribution shows which channels are contributing to pipeline across the journey
  • Landing pages matched to segment, channel, and intent: trial, demo, or content by stage
  • Every deal in the CRM has a source path connected to the channels that influenced it
  • CAC tracked by channel, segment, and quarter: budget decisions are based on pipeline data
  • Weekly review cadence: what changed, what to test, what to scale or stop
What VonClaro Fixes

Five parts of the revenue system. All connected.

We design and operate the full revenue system: demand generation, conversion infrastructure, attribution, nurture, and the optimization layer that connects them so performance compounds over time rather than running in parallel with no shared signal.

01

Demand Creation

Paid + SEO + Content

02

Conversion Infrastructure

03

Attribution and CRM Foundation

Attribution + CRM Source Data

Multi-touch attribution connected to your CRM so pipeline source is visible at the deal level. UTM coverage across all paid channels. CRM source fields populated. CAC calculable by channel and segment. Budget decisions made on real signal.

Nurture and Handoff

Email + Sales Alignment

Email sequences triggered by paid and content signals, not just time. Sales handoff logic that routes the right leads at the right moment. No leads sitting in a generic drip while sales waits for qualified pipeline.

05

Optimization Layer

Reporting + Weekly Cadence

Weekly review of what changed and why. Monthly reporting on CAC, pipeline contribution by channel, and conversion rate by segment. Structured experimentation so the system compounds rather than plateaus.
